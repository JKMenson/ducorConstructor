New Construction

At Docor Construction, we specialize in the art of creating from the ground up. Our new construction services encompass the full spectrum of residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Remodeling and Renovation

Transform your existing space into a masterpiece with Docor Construction's remodeling and renovation services. Our experienced team thrives on breathing new life into homes and commercial spaces, updating layouts, enhancing functionality, and bringing a fresh aesthetic appeal. Whether it's a kitchen makeover, a bathroom renovation, or a complete overhaul of living spaces, we approach each project with meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to delivering transformative results that exceed expectations

Desing-Building Services

Experience the convenience and efficiency of a unified approach with Docor Construction's design-build services. By combining architectural vision with construction expertise, we ensure that your project benefits from a cohesive and collaborative approach, resulting in a finished product that aligns perfectly with your vision and objectives
