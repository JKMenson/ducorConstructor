Our Mission

Our mission at Docor Construction is to be the trusted partner in Liberia's construction journey, delivering bespoke solutions that align with our clients' needs and contribute to the nation's progress. We are dedicated to leveraging our expertise, embracing technology, and prioritizing sustainable practices. With integrity at our core, we aim to cultivate enduring relationships with clients, suppliers, and the local community. Through effective project management, a focus on safety, and a commitment to quality, we endeavor to not only construct buildings but to actively participate in the sustainable development and prosperity of Liberia.

Our Vision

At Docor Construction, our vision is to emerge as a cornerstone of Liberia's construction landscape, exemplifying unparalleled excellence, innovation, and sustainable growth. We envision a future where our commitment to quality construction not only shapes skylines but contributes significantly to the socio-economic development of Liberia. Our goal is to be recognized as a key player in advancing infrastructure, promoting local talent, and fostering a resilient built environment that reflects the spirit and aspirations of Liberia.
